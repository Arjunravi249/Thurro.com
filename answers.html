Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Thurro Answers

What types of questions can I ask Thurro Answers?

+

You can ask complex, open-ended questions about Indian companies, markets, and regulatory matters. Examples include financial performance analysis, competitive benchmarking, regulatory compliance questions, market trend analysis, and strategic research queries. Our AI understands context and provides detailed, source-backed responses.

How accurate and reliable are the answers?

+

Thurro Answers maintains 95% accuracy by grounding all responses in verified regulatory filings, official company documents, and curated datasets. Every answer includes direct source links, allowing you to verify information and dive deeper into the original documents.

What data sources does Thurro Answers access?

+

Our platform accesses 800+ data sources including regulatory filings, annual reports, earnings transcripts, market data, alternative datasets, and real-time news. We focus on India's deepest repository of corporate and financial data, all UPSI/MNPI-free and continuously updated.

How do Notebooks work?

+

Every question automatically creates a Notebook—a dedicated research thread. You can follow suggested questions to explore topics deeper, add your own queries, and build comprehensive research narratives. Notebooks are shareable and allow collaborative research with full version history.

What are Tasks and how do they save time?

+

Tasks automate repetitive research workflows. Define your research parameters once, and Tasks will automatically generate structured reports across multiple companies or themes. This transforms hours of manual research into streamlined, repeatable processes perfect for regular market monitoring or comparative analysis.

Is my research data secure and private?

+

Yes, absolutely. We use advanced encryption and maintain strict data privacy standards. Your research queries, Notebooks, and insights remain private to your account. We operate an UPSI/MNPI-free environment and ensure all data handling meets financial industry compliance requirements.

Can I integrate Thurro Answers with my existing workflow?

+

Yes, Thurro Answers is designed for seamless integration. You can export findings with full source attribution, share Notebooks with team members, and access our platform alongside your existing research tools. We also offer API access for custom integrations with enterprise workflows.

How often is the data updated?

+

Our data is continuously updated in real-time. New regulatory filings, earnings announcements, and market data are processed and indexed as they become available. You can refresh any analysis with one click to incorporate the latest information while preserving historical context.
