Success Story

Custom Report for NIIF

The toll plazas in India collect tolls via a digital process. Indian Highways Management Corporation Limited releases data on the number of vehicles and tolls collected at each location. For the National Investment and Infrastructure Fund (NIIF), Thurro visualised the toll plazas on the map of India with colour coding showing the level of growth at each particular plaza.

Such granular data is extremely useful to understand the location of economic activity and growth. Creative analytics like these allow asset managers, investors, and policy makers a quick snapshot of the underlying infrastructure assets, enabling informed decision-making and strategic planning.
