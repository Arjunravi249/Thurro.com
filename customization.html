Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Thurro's customisation services

How do I get started with Thurro's customisation services?

+

Contact our expert team to discuss your specific intelligence needs. We'll work with you to identify the best combination of custom datasets, reports, and API feeds for your business objectives. You can begin by clicking 'Start your custom journey' below to schedule a consultation, or contact our team directly via email.

What are custom datasets?

+

Custom datasets are specially curated and structured data collections tailored to your specific research and analysis needs. Our expert team collaborates with you to identify, gather, and organise data that aligns perfectly with your requirements. These can include proprietary financial metrics, industry-specific indicators, or any specialized data points crucial to your business intelligence.

Can I access proprietary data through custom datasets?

+

Yes, Thurro offers paid and proprietary data bundles for datasets where we have exclusive access, providing you with unique intelligence advantages. These proprietary datasets cover various sectors and metrics not easily available elsewhere, giving you deeper insights into market trends and competitive positioning.

What types of custom reports can you create?

+

We can design various types of reports including district-level indicators on maps, portfolio analyses, competitor public data reports, immediate AI responses to market events, and any other format that provides the specific insights you require. All reports can be tailored to your organization's presentation style and analytical needs.

Can reports be automated for regular delivery?

+

Absolutely. We can automate the delivery of custom reports directly to your inbox, ensuring you receive timely, tailored insights without missing critical information. Set up daily, weekly, or monthly delivery schedules, or trigger reports based on specific market events or thresholds.

What is a custom API feed?

+

A custom API feed provides real-time data tailored to your business needs through seamless API integration. This allows you to access Thurro's intelligence directly within your existing systems and applications, enabling automated workflows and integrated analytics without switching between platforms.

What branding or white labeling options are available?

+

We can customize the URL of our AI platform to reflect your client portal alongside Thurro branding, creating a seamless experience for your users. Through our white labeling and custom watermarking options, you can present Thurro intelligence as part of your own branded offerings. Reports can also be fully customized with your organization's prompts, fonts, tables, charts, and branding, and downloaded with your watermark and without reference to Thurro.

Can Thurro AI be integrated with our existing internal data?

+

Yes, we offer the option to layer Thurro AI capabilities on top of your institutional data, allowing you to extract deeper insights from your proprietary information. This integration preserves data security while enhancing analytical capabilities through our advanced AI models.

What specialized NowCast options are available?

+

We can create specific NowCast models tailored to your industry or focus areas, providing real-time predictive analytics for the metrics that matter most to your business. Additionally, we offer NowCast deepdive services to help you understand the methodology behind our predictive models and how they can be optimized for your specific use cases.

How is pricing determined for custom services?

+

Pricing varies based on the complexity and scope of customisation required. Factors include the types of customization needed, data volume, refresh frequency, and integration complexity. Contact our team for a detailed quote based on your specific needs and to discuss flexible pricing options that align with your budget.
